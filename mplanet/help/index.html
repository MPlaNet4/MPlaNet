Multiple Platform Network Assistant

MPlaNet is designed specifically with those LANs lans in mind, that have machines running on different platforms. In that case, usually it becomes difficult to ask for remote assistance or file sharing or even chatting.

MPlaNet overcomes all these problems to give you an easy way for networking across different operating systems. Now you can work on your friend's computer with your monitor, mouse and keyboard as if his computer is yours. You can go through his files and folders as if they are yours and you can chat with him.
