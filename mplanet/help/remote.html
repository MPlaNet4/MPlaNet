Remote Assistance

If you need help from someone, click on the icon of the user from whom you need help to select him and then click the ‘Remote Assistance’ button on the toolbar. A message will be displayed on that user’s screen telling him that you are asking assistance from him. He can opt to either help you or he can turn down your request. A message will be displayed on your screen according to that user’s selection.

If he agrees to help you, then your current screenshot will be displayed on his screen. From then onwards, he can work on your computer using his monitor, keyboard and mouse. Remote assistance will stop when one of you opts to stop it by clicking the ‘Remote Assistance’ button on your MPlaNet window.

Though the button for starting and stopping remote assistance is same, you can make out when to start or stop remote assistance by the looks of the button and the tool tip that you get when to take your mouse pointer on that button. When no assistance is going on, the tool tip will show ‘Remote Assistance’ and the button will not have ‘Disable’ icon. Once remote assistance starts, the tool tip will show ‘Stop Remote Assistance’ and the button will show the ‘Disable’ icon at the right bottom corner.
